Fresh Salmon with Thai Noodles

Ingredients:

1. 2 salmon fillets

2. 200g of egg or rice noodles

3. 1 tsp fish sauce

4. 1 tsp of olive oil

5. 4 large garlic cloves, minced

6. 1 large shallot, thinly sliced

7. 2-3 tsp of Thai red curry paste

8. 200ml vegetable or chicken stock

9. 1/2 small bunch coriander, roughly chopped

Instructions:

1. Cook the noodles following the pack instructions, rinse and drain.

2. Heat the oil in a deep saucepan and fry the curry paste and garlice for 1 min until fragrant. Add the stock, fish sauce, spring onions, and bring quickly to a boil and simmer for 5 mins. Add the salmon pieces and cook for 2 mins, then stir in half the coriander.

3. Divide the noodles between 4 bowls and spoon over the salmon and sauce. Sprinkle over the remaining coriander and shallots.
