Fluffy Morning Pancakes

Ingredients:

1. 2 1/2 tsps baking powder

2. 2 large eggs

3. 225g plain flour

4. 1 pinch of salt

5. 300ml of milk

6. 30 grams butter

7. Bananas, strawberries (optional)

Instructions:

1. The easiest way to make these is to put all the ingredients into a blender and blitz. But if you do mix up the batter by hand in a bowl, make a well in the flour, baking powder, salt, beat in the eggs, melted butter and milk, and transfer to a jug: it's much easier to pour the batter into the pan than to spoon it.

2. Heat a smooth griddle or pan on the stove.

3. When you cook the pancakes, all you need to remember is that when the upper side of the pancake is blistering and bubbling it's time to cook the second side, and this needs only about 1 minute, if that. This recipe will produce 16 silver-dollar-sized pancakes.

4. Once ready, present on a plate and pour maple or chocolate syrup over the pancakes. Add optional toppings for desired taste. It is ready to be devoured!
